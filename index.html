The internet increases the productivity of high income workers and decreases that of low income workers
High income workers use the internet for productivity

Low income workers use it for entertainment
Low income workers use the internet later at night
Methods
Assumption 1: During the night (10pm to 4am) traffic generated in a tile is generated only by residents.

Motivation: Use of socio-demographic characteristics of residents
Assumption 2 (Strong): Using an app for one minute always generates the same amount of traffic.

Motivation: Transforming app traffic into usage time
Assumption 2: The amount of traffic generated by an app per minute has a known distribution.

  • App traffic distribution
  • Sample traffic per minute
Assumption 3: When individual spend time online they use apps from the NetMob dataset

Assumption 4: 3G internet usage patterns are representative
Further robustness checks: Noise and Mobility
Issue 1: What we care about is absolute time spent online, but we only have relative quantities

Issue 2: Causality -- Do low income households stay up later/consume more entertainment because of the internet?
